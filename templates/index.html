Welcome to UTM Converter

This tool converts UTM coordinates with known Easting, Northing and Zone values. Instantly convert your data into Latitude/Longitude (decimal degrees) or Degrees, Minutes, Seconds (DMS) formats.

Easting is the X-coordinate in the UTM system. It tells you how far east (in meters) a location is from the central meridian of its UTM zone.

Northing is the Y-coordinate. It tells you how far north (in meters) a location is from the equator.

📍 UTM to DMS

Convert Universal Transverse Mercator (UTM) coordinates into Degrees, Minutes, Seconds (DMS). Ideal for manual plotting or sharing in traditional formats.

Go to UTM → DMS

🌐 UTM to Latitude/Longitude

Convert UTM coordinates to standard latitude and longitude format (decimal degrees). Useful for GPS devices, mapping apps, and geospatial analysis.

Go to UTM → LatLon

📘 How to Use

  1. Select a conversion type above.
  2. Enter your Easting, Northing, and Zone Longitude.
  3. Click “Convert” to see the result instantly.
